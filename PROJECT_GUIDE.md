# 🌸 Bloom: The Simple Guide

Welcome to **Bloom**, a safe space for students to share their voices without the fear of judgment.

## 1. What is Bloom?
Imagine a digital community where you can share your deepest secrets, funniest stories, or strongest opinions. You can say what you want, but **nobody knows it's you.**

It’s like a community garden where every voice is a flower, and everyone is anonymous.

## 2. How it Works (Step-by-Step)
1.  **Log In**: Use your university email. This proves you are a student, which keeps the community safe.
2.  **Identity Shield**: The moment you log in, the system "hides" your real name. It never connects your email to what you write.
3.  **Get a Name**: When you post, the app gives you a random name like *"Toxic Ivy"*, *"Midnight Rose"*, or *"Golden Daisy"*.
4.  **Share**: Write a story, a confession, or an opinion. You can even add tags or react to others.

## 3. Why is it Safe?
*   **Total Anonymity**: We use a special database trick that makes it impossible to trace a post back to a specific person.
*   **Auto-Moderation**: If someone is being mean or sharing private info (like phone numbers), the "Bloom Robot" (our moderation script) will catch it and hide it.
*   **Report System**: If a post gets too many reports, it disappears until an admin checks it.

## 4. The "Cool" Features
*   **Story Canvas**: A beautiful, book-style way to read posts.
*   **Reactions**: Like or love posts to show support.
*   **Privacy First**: No tracking, no ads, no judgment.

## 5. For the Curious (Under the Hood)
*   **Framework**: Next.js (Fast and Modern)
*   **Database**: MongoDB (Flexible and Scalable)
*   **Auth**: NextAuth (Secure Logins)
*   **Styling**: Premium custom CSS for a high-end, "glassy" look.
